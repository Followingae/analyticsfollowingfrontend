## Design Context

### Users
Marketing managers and brand strategists at agencies and brands (25-45 years old) who need Instagram influencer analytics for campaign planning. They work in fast-paced environments, evaluating creators, building proposals, and tracking campaign ROI. Secondary audience: superadmins managing the platform operations.

### Brand Personality
**Professional, Confident, Refined** — The interface should feel like a premium SaaS tool that inspires trust. Not playful or casual — this is a business tool handling real money (campaigns, pricing, billing).

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Tone**: Refined minimal with purposeful depth. Clean surfaces, precise typography, subtle layering.
- **Theme**: Light and dark mode via OKLCH color system. Primary hue at ~303° (violet-purple). Neutral tints toward the brand hue.
- **Typography**: Geist font family. Large display headings (text-4xl+), tight tracking on titles, regular weight body text.
- **Surfaces**: Glass-morphism inputs (`GlassInputWrapper`), rounded-2xl corners on interactive elements, subtle shadows. Cards use `bg-card` with border, no heavy shadows.
- **Animations**: Staggered entrance animations (`animate-element animate-delay-*`), smooth transitions (150ms), no heavy motion.
- **Anti-references**: Generic dashboard templates, heavy gradients, neon-on-dark "AI aesthetic", cramped data tables. NO Inter/Roboto fonts.
- **References**: Linear.app (precision), Stripe Dashboard (clarity), Vercel (confidence in space).

### Design Principles
1. **Breathe** — Generous whitespace. Not everything needs a card. Let content stand on its own.
2. **One thing per view** — Each page has one clear purpose. Guide the eye with hierarchy, not decoration.
3. **Earned complexity** — Simple by default, detailed on demand. Progressive disclosure over overwhelming dashboards.
4. **Consistent language** — Same patterns for same actions. Glass inputs, rounded-2xl buttons, staggered animations.
5. **Dark mode is not an afterthought** — Every color, shadow, and surface must be intentional in both modes.
